# Content rules for useful Xiaohongshu knowledge sharing

## The operating principle

The post earns attention with a specific result, then earns trust by explaining the practical action behind it. It does not use an abstract AI introduction as its main idea.

Use this sequence:

`specific result or familiar problem -> real work scene -> one practical AI-assisted action -> verified outcome or usable takeaway -> one discussion question`

## Select a topic

Choose one of these evidence-backed starting points:

- a published business result with a number, time span, and business scene;
- a common work task that a person can recognize immediately;
- a public open-source project, explained through what it helps a person do and how to begin using it.

Store the source, verification date, assumptions, and any caveats in the internal asset record. Do not turn a simulated scenario into a customer case.

## Write for ordinary readers

- Prefer “自动把客户和订单放在一起看” over “multi-source data orchestration”.
- Prefer “先把复杂工作准备好，最后由人判断” over “autonomous agent decision-making”.
- Describe a person, time, task, and consequence before explaining a tool.
- Keep a page to one point and roughly 30–50 Chinese characters where the design permits.
- Avoid English abbreviations and specialist terms unless the reader needs them to use the item; explain them in the same sentence.

## Packaging rules

- Final title: no more than 20 Chinese characters; use a concrete result, tension, benefit, or curiosity cue without inventing a claim.
- Cover: state one clear benefit or conflict; it must be legible at thumbnail size.
- Pages 2–6: explain the scene, old friction, practical action, and takeaway in a readable order.
- Final page: ask one specific question that invites a reader to share their own work problem.
- Use audience and problem tags, not only broad AI tags.

## Public-post boundary

Do not place source citations, commercial quotations, delivery commitments, internal process diagrams, credentials, personal data, or exaggerated guarantees in the public post by default. Keep evidence and compliance notes in the internal record so claims remain auditable.

## Approved-copy lock

After the user approves a version, treat title, page copy, body, and tags as a locked publishing artifact. Compare generated images and form fields to that artifact before upload. If a change is necessary, surface the exact before-and-after text for approval.
